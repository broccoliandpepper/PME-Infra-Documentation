# Microsoft 365 Business Premium

## Overview

Microsoft 365 Business Premium is the primary productivity licence assigned to all K Creation & Production users.

It provides access to essential business applications, collaboration tools, and cloud services required across the entire organisation.

## Included Services

### Office Applications

- **Microsoft Word**: Document creation and editing.
- **Microsoft Excel**: Spreadsheet and data analysis.
- **Microsoft PowerPoint**: Presentation creation.
- **Microsoft Access**: Database management (as included).
- **Microsoft Publisher**: Desktop publishing (as included).
- **Microsoft OneNote**: Note-taking and collaboration.

### Communication & Collaboration

- **Microsoft Teams**: Instant messaging, video conferencing, team collaboration.
- **Outlook**: Email, calendar, and contact management (cloud-based).

### Storage & File Sharing

- **SharePoint Online**: Central document repository and team site collaboration.
- **OneDrive for Business**: Personal cloud storage for each user.

### Additional Services

- **Microsoft Forms**: Survey and poll creation.
- **Microsoft Stream**: Video hosting and streaming (if enabled).
- **Microsoft To Do**: Task management.
- **Power BI (limited)**: Basic analytics (depending on plan specifics).

## Deployment & Management

All Microsoft 365 Business Premium licences are:

- Assigned centrally via Entra ID.
- Managed through Microsoft 365 admin centre.
- Deployed to devices via Intune.

### User Experience

- Users access Microsoft 365 services via:
  - **Web browser**: Office.com portal.
  - **Desktop applications**: Office suite installed via Intune.
  - **Mobile**: Office mobile apps (if provisioned).

## Security Features Included

Microsoft 365 Business Premium includes:

- **Defender for Office 365**: Email and collaboration threat protection.
- **Data Loss Prevention (DLP)**: Prevent sensitive data exfiltration.
- **Advanced Threat Protection**: Protection against phishing and malware.
- **Conditional Access integration**: Access policies based on device and user risk.

## Scope Across Departments

All K Creation & Production departments use Microsoft 365 Business Premium:

- **Accounting**: Excel spreadsheets, Teams for finance collaboration, Outlook.
- **HR**: Forms for surveys, Teams for announcements, SharePoint for policies.
- **Production**: Teams for coordination, SharePoint for production documents, Word for reports.
- **Design/Proofing**: Teams for feedback, SharePoint for design files, Office apps.

## Cost Efficiency

By standardising on Microsoft 365 Business Premium:

- Single licensing model across the organisation.
- Integrated with existing cloud infrastructure (Azure, Entra ID, Intune).
- Reduces need for multiple point solutions.
- Simplifies user onboarding and management.

## Integration with Infrastructure

Microsoft 365 Business Premium is the foundation for:

- **Intune management**: Devices are managed using Business Premium policies.
- **Entra ID**: User identities and conditional access control access to M365 services.
- **Defender for Endpoint P2**: Integrates with M365 to provide unified threat protection.
- **SharePoint Online backup**: All SharePoint Online data is backed up via QNAP and Azure Storage.
